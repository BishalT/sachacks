A BIT ABOUT US

SacHacks is the first major collegiate hackathon in the Sacramento, California area. Our passion is to create opportunities for those with potential to launch their ideas in a public venue. We provide the place, you bring your ideas, and we have a friendly competition between all creative coders to launch their ideas into action during a 36-hour hackathon.

Food and refreshments will be provided by our team. Just bring yourself and your laptop! And maybe your sleepingbag.

FAQ

  • What is hackathon

    A hackathon occurs when passionate people gather together to create, build, and launch their ideas with tech.

  • How much does it cost?

    Everything is provided by our team and sponsors. Event is free to attend.

  • Who can attend?

    Any undergraduate student, graduate student, or industry professionals over the age of 18 are welcome!

  • Code of Conduct?

    All participants of our event must follow the MLH Code of Conduct. Click to download pdf for more information: MLH Code of Conduct PDF

  • I’m not the best coder. Can I still attend?

    Yes, we embrace diversity. It will be a great opporunity to pick up new skills from other coders at the event. Applications are not filtered based on experience.

  • I don’t have a team. Can I still attend?

    Yes! We will have a team mixer for hackers to form teams.
