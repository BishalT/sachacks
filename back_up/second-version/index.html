A BIT ABOUT US

SacHacks is the first major collegiate hackathon in the Sacramento, California area. Our passion is to create opportunities for those with potential to launch their ideas in a public venue. We provide the place, you bring your ideas, and we have a friendly competition between all creative coders to launch their ideas into action during a 36-hour hackathon.

Food and refreshments will be provided by our team. Just bring yourself and your laptop! And maybe your sleepingbag.
