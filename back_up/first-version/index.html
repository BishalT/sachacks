WHO WE ARE

SacHacks is the first major collegiate hackathon in the Sacramento, California area. We work to provide an accessible venue for creative coders to launch their ideas into action during a 36-hour hackathon.

Food and refreshments will be provided by our team. Just bring yourself and your passion!
